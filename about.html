About AFPP

Founded in 2024, Amritsar Food and Parantha Point (AFPP) was born from a simple dream — to bring the authentic flavors of Indian food to every plate. What started as a small kitchen serving homestyle parathas and tiffin meals soon grew into a trusted food destination known for its warmth, quality, and tradition.

At AFPP, every dish tells a story — of the streets of Amritsar, the aroma of fresh ghee, and the love that goes into home-cooked food. From buttery parathas and rich Chole Bhature to hearty thalis and evening chai-time snacks, our menu celebrates the essence of North Indian comfort food.

We believe in serving fresh, flavorful, and fulfilling meals that make you feel at home, no matter where you are. Whether you’re stopping by for breakfast or ordering a cozy dinner, AFPP is here to serve you happiness — one bite at a time.
